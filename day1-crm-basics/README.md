# Day 1 - CRM Basics

## What is CRM?
CRM is a system used to manage customer relationships.

## Why companies use Salesforce
- Manage customer data
- Track sales
- Improve customer service

## Salesforce Objects

### Account
Represents a company or organization.

### Contact
Represents a person.

### Opportunity
Represents a potential deal.

## Business Workflow
Lead → Contact → Opportunity → Customer

## Real-Life Mapping

### College Admission

Account = College
Contact = Student
Lead = Student Inquiry
Opportunity = Admission Process
